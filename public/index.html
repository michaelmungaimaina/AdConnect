Our Privacy Policy

Effective: December 20th, 2024

At AdConnect, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you engage with our services, visit our website, or interact with our digital platforms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of:
- Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide when filling out forms on our website.
- Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior through cookies and tracking technologies (e.g., Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel).
- Device Information: Device type, screen resolution, and geographic location (if shared).

2. How We Use Your Data

We collect and use your data for the following purposes:
- To connect real estate companies with potential local and international customers.
- To analyze website performance and improve user experience using tools like Google Analytics.
- To create targeted and personalized marketing campaigns through Facebook Pixel and similar tools.
- To send updates, promotional offers, and service-related communications.
- To respond to your inquiries or requests.

3. Sharing Your Data

We may share your data with third parties, such as:
- Marketing Platforms: Facebook, Google, and other advertising partners to enable social media advertising campaigns.
- Service Providers: Platforms used for email marketing, analytics, or lead management.
- We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties for their own purposes.

4. Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:
- Understand user behavior and preferences on our website.
- Serve relevant ads to users based on their browsing history.
- Measure the performance of our marketing campaigns.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Retention

We store personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

6. Security of Your Data

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal i nformation from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse. However, no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:
- Access: Request access to your personal data and information about how we process it.
- Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
- Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal requirements.
- Opt-Out: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us.

8. International Users

If you are accessing our services from outside Kenya, please note that your information may be transferred, stored, and processed in Kenya. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
